About Nebras

Nebras is built to support structured transcription sessions. Users can record or upload audio, convert it into text (via AI), then review, refine, and store it for later access.

Key Features

Live Mode

Record from your microphone and work in an editor.

Upload Mode

Upload audio files and manage transcript sessions.

Session Management

Save, open, and delete sessions from your workspace.
